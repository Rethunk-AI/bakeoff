RE: model_id UUID - conflict detection + multi-source coalescing: if the primary already exists, we should also verify the other fields are not changed (perhaps a new version), and if so, consider how to best merge the data.  Maybe this is a simple update to the descrioption, or maybe the creator's contact information has changed (new homepage, etc); these are both acceptable updates.  Or maybe the submitter wants to forge information by changing context length or parameter counts; these would be detected by the validation system as not matching the weights and published information and it should probably reject the source as well (since it also is likely invalid) with a message clearly indicating why, but it should not remove the file because the user may have made a mistake and needs to fix a typo or they may be actively editing the file, and in either case, we don't want them to lose their data.  They always have the option to resubmit the failed request to try again.

Other than that, I agree with your explanations and approaches described in your latest comment here.
